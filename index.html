02 — Design Concept
Design Rationale

The mark that
earns its meaning.

The Command Center
Top Cabin

Think of this as the strategic brain behind the operation. Just like a control room, this sturdy upper section represents a well-thought-out architecture and structured logic. It stands for the clear vision and precise planning that must happen before any real execution takes place.

The Driving Foundation
Bottom-Left Tracks

This solid, horizontal base symbolizes resilience — both in our infrastructure and our work ethic. It's the quiet but powerful engine running behind the scenes. Built to handle heavy workloads and push through complex technical barriers, it ensures progress never stagnates and we keep moving forward relentlessly.

The Execution Edge
Bottom-Right Bucket

The sharp, forward-facing angle is all about precision and impact. Much like an excavator digging for valuable material, this element reflects our ability to cut through the noise, tackle the core of a problem, and extract the most valuable, refined solutions directly into the hands of our users.

HIMAC ATTACHMENTS Heavy Equipment · Australia
01

What They Make

HIMAC Attachments manufactures premium-grade excavator attachments — buckets, rippers, grabs, and compaction plates — engineered for Australian conditions and exported globally.

02

Where They Stand

Not a volume brand. HIMAC holds a position built on durability and precision fit. Buyers are operators who run machines hard and need parts that don't fail on a job site.

03

What This Brand Says

Capable. Unambiguous. Grounded in the work itself. The identity doesn't reach for anything the product can't back up — exactly the right move for this industry.

05 — Usage Guidelines

Do's

✓ Do
On dark backgrounds
Light version only. Full clear space maintained. No shadows or effects.
✓ Do
On yellow backgrounds
Dark version only on yellow. The only approved logo for this surface.
✓ Do
On white / light backgrounds
Dark version on light. Proportions locked — no stretching, no modification.
05 — Do's
06 — Usage Guidelines

Don'ts

✗ Don't
Don't stretch or distort
Geometric proportions are locked. Never alter width, height, or skew.
✗ Don't
Don't use off-brand backgrounds
Only dark, yellow, or white surfaces. No gradients, patterns, or off-brand colors.
✗ Don't
Don't rotate
The mark must always sit level. Rotation at any angle is forbidden.
